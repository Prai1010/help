New York City

The Big Apple. Known for its bustling city environment, has much to choose from. From popular tourist locations the Empire State Building, One World Trade Center, or a walk simple through Central Park— let New York speak to you!

Tourist Attractions

Explore the most popular attractions!

Tourist Attractions

NYC is packed with world-famous landmarks and must-see spots. Here are our recommendations:

  • Statue of Liberty — arguably the most iconic symbol of freedom on Liberty Island, though technically in New Jersey...
  • Central Park — the iconic large urban park perfect for walks, picnics, and people-watching
  • Empire State Building — the classic skyscraper with panoramic city views, also gives a wonderful view of Central Park
  • Times Square — offering the most bustling vibes, bright lights, theaters, entertainment and more

Good Eats

New York is a global food capital, offering everything from street eats to fine dining:

  • Katz's Delicatessen — a legendary spot for pastrami sandwiches and other deli
  • Joe's Pizza — offering classic New York style pizza by the slice for a bargain
  • Friedmans — with locations all over, the family run restaurant offfers delicious eats — all gluten-free
  • Levain Bakery — bakery famous for a multitude of pastries such as their thick and gooey cookies

Cultural Activities

Transportation

Save money and go public

Transportation

New York's public transit makes it almost too easy to see all there is to see. Here are some of our recommendations:

  • Subway - it's fast, afforadble and connects all boroughs using metrocards is fun, but after the first few tap-to-pay's your remaining rides are free
  • Buses — reliable and easy to navigate, very convenient when consistently traveling within certain areas, free tap applies here too
  • Citi Bike — a bike-share system for shorter rides that you can take across the city
  • Walking — as crazy as it may sound, many things are a walkable distance when you use your map properly
